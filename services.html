Services

AH Barcelona is a one-stop shop company for apartment owners that use their apartments for short-term rentals and want to provide their guests with the highest level of service. Just provide us with the check-in client information and AH Barcelona will take care of the rest. Whether your apartment is rented out every week or every few months – AH will make sure that it is in the best possible condition for the guests to enjoy their time in Barcelona. We are also happy to provide additional concierge services upon request.

Easy Communication

  • We collect information electronically from the booking, and communicate with apartments owners through this system
  • Owners are kept updated about any changes in guest stays, and are notified when the apartment is ready for the next guest
