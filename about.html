About

Established in 2011 by Gustavo Scaglusi, AH Barcelona started out providing check-in and cleaning services for only one apartment in Barcelona. Today, AH provides a comprehensive array of hosting services, including check-in, check-out, cleaning, laundry and concierge, to over 40 apartments throughout Barcelona. Our staff is trained to perform the highest levels of service, even for apartments with high turnover. Our check-in personnel speak English and can engage with the renting client for a unique welcoming experience. Our cleaning and fixing staff is also trained and constantly evaluated for optimal performance. Please refer to our testimonials to gauge the level of customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to ensure that renting your apartment becomes a seamless easy process - you get us the renting client, we'll take care of the rest.
